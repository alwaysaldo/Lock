Features

Locked-In Wagers

Once everyone locks in, the wager is set. No backing out. The platform tracks everything so nobody can ghost.

Any Team Size

1v1, 2v2, 3v2 with odds, 4v4, 5v5 — whatever the matchup is. Uneven teams with custom odds fully supported.

Stats & Leaderboard

Win rate, earnings, best teammate combos, win streaks — all tracked automatically across your squad.
